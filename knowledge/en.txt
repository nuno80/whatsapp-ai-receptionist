THE ROOM
========
B&B Roma — a newly renovated private room at Via Luigi Rizzo 36, Rome.
Just 600 metres from the Vatican Museums, an 8-minute walk.
Prati neighbourhood, one of the most elegant and safe areas in Rome.

The room has a double bed and accommodates up to 2 guests.

LOCATION & HOW TO GET HERE
============================
Address: Via Luigi Rizzo 36, Rome (Prati area).
- Vatican Museums: 600m on foot (8 minutes)
- St. Peter's Basilica: 900m on foot (12 minutes)
- Castel Sant'Angelo: 1 km on foot (15 minutes)
- Cipro metro station (line A): 5 minutes on foot
- Ottaviano metro station (line A): 8 minutes on foot
- Roma Termini station: 20 minutes by metro line A
- Fiumicino Airport: Leonardo Express train to Termini, then metro A to Cipro

SURROUNDINGS & TIPS
====================
The Prati neighbourhood offers excellent restaurants, bars and shops within walking distance.
The covered market on Via Cola di Rienzo is great for grocery shopping.
